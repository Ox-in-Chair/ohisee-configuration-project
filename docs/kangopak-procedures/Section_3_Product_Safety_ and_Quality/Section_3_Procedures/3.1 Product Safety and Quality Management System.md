**Status:** Current

## Scope

This is not a procedure but serves to describe the Product Safety and Quality Management System.

## Purpose

The purpose of this document is as above and is a brief outline of the structure of the Product Safety and Quality Management System in accordance with BRC Global Standard for Packaging and Packaging Materials.

All management systems and HACCP policies, procedures and records as defined in the BRC manual indices.

## Authority

The Administration Manager is responsible for the maintenance of the management system and is authorised to make changes to the manual, as and when, required.

## Procedure

The BRC Food Safety and Quality management system is structured as follows:

- An overall policy statement regarding the intention to maintain customer satisfaction, legislation, food safety and quality acceptability exists and has been communicated to all employees
- Other general policies regarding Human Resources issues
- Procedures covering the requirements of the BRC Global Standard for Packaging and Packaging Materials. Specifications for packaging components, raw materials used in the manufacture of packaging and product identification specification sheets
- Records demonstrating compliance to legislation and the BRC Global Standard for Packaging and Packaging Materials

All procedures are aligned to reflect the clauses of the BRC Global Standard for Packaging and Packaging Materials and have been numbered as such, where possible.

Procedures are referenced to each other, where possible, as defined under the referencing section of each procedure.

Records relating to each procedure are defined in the actual procedure and numbered as closely as possible to the document number.
