---

## SHARPS POLICY

It is our Policy to take all practicable steps to prevent the contamination of food products/packaging by sharps or any foreign objects. The foreign body control procedure provides details on the controls required in the unlikely event of glass breakage or contamination.

## WE ENSURE

- Sharp blades, equipment and tools are not left in a position that allows them to contaminate the product

- Sharp cutting instruments used in the manufacture of packaging materials are controlled to prevent product contamination. This includes control into and out of the factory

- Snap-off blades knives are not used

- Where open notice boards are present in the production, packing and storage areas. Loose fastening, such as drawing pins and stables, are not used

---

## Implementation Notes

This policy statement establishes the organization's commitment to preventing contamination by sharps and foreign objects through controlled handling and storage procedures.
