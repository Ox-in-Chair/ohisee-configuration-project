# Procedure 5.8.3.1: Water Disruption Policy

**Company:** Kangopak
**Document Type:** Emergency Policy
**Revision:** 0
**Revised Date:** 2025-09-02
**Document Owner:** Operations Manager
**Status:** Current

---

## OBJECTIVE

To ensure product safety and maintain hygiene standards during the water supply disruption from 24 June 2024 to 26 June 2024 while ensuring that all employees adhere strictly to company rules.

## SCOPE

This policy applies to all Kangopak employees and facilities affected by the scheduled water disruption.

## RESPONSIBILITIES

**Employees:** Employees must bring their drinking water and water for the coffee machine. Employees are also responsible for using the provided non-potable water to flush toilets.

**Management:** Ensure availability of non-potable water for toilets and hand sanitiser at all essential points within the facility.

## GUIDELINES

### Drinking Water and Coffee Machine

- All employees must bring sufficient drinking water throughout the day
- Employees are also responsible for bringing water to the coffee machine
- Failure to comply will result in disciplinary action

### Toilet Flushing

- Non-potable water will be provided by Kangopak for flushing toilets. Employees must use this water responsibly
- Employees found not adhering to this will face disciplinary measures

### Hygiene Maintenance

- Hand sanitiser will be available at all machines, in the toilets, and in the canteen. Employees must use hand sanitiser frequently to maintain hygiene standards
- Neglecting hygiene protocols will lead to disciplinary action

### Absenteeism

- The water disruption is not an excuse for absenteeism. All employees are expected to adhere to their normal working schedule
- Unjustified absenteeism will be dealt with severely

### Maintenance of Product Safety

- Operations must ensure that the production processes are not compromised. Regular checks and audits should be performed to ensure that the water disruption does not affect product quality

## POLICY ENFORCEMENT

- All employees are responsible for complying with this policy
- Kangopak will not pick up after any employee. Each employee must take personal responsibility for their compliance
- Non-compliance with any part of this policy will result in disciplinary action

## COMMUNICATION

This policy must be communicated to all employees by the end of the day on 23 June 2024.

## ACKNOWLEDGEMENT

Employees must acknowledge that they have read, understood, and will comply with this policy. Failure to acknowledge does not exempt from compliance and disciplinary actions.

---

## Implementation Notes

This policy provides emergency procedures for maintaining operations and hygiene standards during water supply disruptions while ensuring employee compliance and product safety.
