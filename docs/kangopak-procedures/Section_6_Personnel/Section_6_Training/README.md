# BRCGS Training Pack for Kangopak Factory Staff

**Version:** 2.0  
**Date:** September 2025  
**Target Audience:** Factory personnel aged 18+  
**Language:** South African English  

## About This Training Pack

This comprehensive training pack converts BRCGS Packaging Materials Issue 7 requirements into accessible training materials for Kangopak factory staff. Each module is designed for classroom delivery and includes practical factory examples, simple activities, and checkpoint questions.

## How to Use This Pack

- Each training module is standalone and classroom-ready
- Modules are organised by training tier (annual mandatory through to operational)
- All content references specific BRCGS clauses and Kangopak procedures
- Training can be delivered in any order based on role requirements

## Standardization Changes Applied

**Version 2.0 Updates:**
- Added Kangopak procedure number header to all modules
- Removed duration lines from all modules  
- Removed revision numbers (Rev X) from procedure references
- Replaced "Food Safety" with "Product Safety" throughout
- Replaced standalone "BRC" with "BRCGS" throughout
- Updated UK legal requirements to South African legal framework
- Removed training signature lines from all modules
- Implemented new file naming convention: BRCGS_Training_[ProcedureNumber]_[ShortTopic].md

**South African Legal Framework:**
- Foodstuffs, Cosmetics and Disinfectants Act 54 of 1972
- Regulations Governing General Hygiene Requirements for Food Premises and the Transport of Food (R962 of 2012)
- Consumer Protection Act 68 of 2008
- National Health Act 61 of 2003
- Occupational Health and Safety Act 85 of 1993
- Basic Conditions of Employment Act 75 of 1997
- Companies Act 71 of 2008
