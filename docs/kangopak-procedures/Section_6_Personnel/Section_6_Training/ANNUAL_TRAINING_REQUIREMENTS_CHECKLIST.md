## PURPOSE

This checklist provides a comprehensive overview of all training requirements that must be completed annually according to BRCGS Packaging Materials Issue 7 and Kangopak procedures. It ensures systematic compliance with mandatory refresher training requirements and maintains certification validity.

---

## REGULATORY FRAMEWORK

### BRCGS Definition of "Annual Frequency"
- Training must be completed within **12 months** since last conducted
- Not based on calendar year - based on actual completion dates
- Records must demonstrate continuous competency maintenance

### Key BRCGS Clauses Requiring Annual Training
- **BRCGS 6.1.6:** "The site shall routinely review and document the competencies of all personnel and provide relevant training as appropriate"
- **BRCGS 6.2.1:** Personal hygiene requirements based on risk assessment
- **Training Records:** Must be maintained for minimum 3 years (BRCGS requirement)

## COMPLIANCE MONITORING FRAMEWORK

### **TRACKING REQUIREMENTS**
- **Individual Training Records:** Date completed, duration, trainer, competency assessment result
- **Annual Compliance Rate:** Must maintain 100% for certification
- **Refresher Scheduling:** Automated alerts 30 days before expiry
- **Competency Verification:** Annual assessment required, not just attendance

### **AUDIT TRAIL MAINTENANCE**
- **Training Certificates:** Individual certificates for each completed module
- **Skills Assessments:** Practical demonstrations + written assessments
- **Training Records:** Maintained for minimum 3 years per BRCGS requirements
- **Management Review:** Annual review of training effectiveness and compliance rates

### **NON-COMPLIANCE ESCALATION**
- **30 days overdue:** Supervisor notification and immediate scheduling
- **60 days overdue:** Management escalation and disciplinary action
- **90 days overdue:** Role restriction until training completed
- **Audit Impact:** Any lapsed training = major non-conformity risk
