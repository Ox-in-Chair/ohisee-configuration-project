## ğŸ“„ PRINT PRODUCTION STANDARDS

### **Document Format Requirements**
- **Page Size:** A4 portrait (210 Ã— 297 mm)
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI minimum for all elements
- **Color Mode:** CMYK for color elements, Grayscale for text-heavy sections
- **Bleed:** 3mm bleed on all sides for full-color materials
- **Safe Area:** 10mm margin from all edges for text and critical elements
- **File Format:** PDF/X-1a:2001 compliant for professional printing

### **Typography Standards**
- **Headings:** Calibri Bold, 14-18pt (hierarchical sizing)
- **Body Text:** Calibri Regular, 11pt (minimum for readability)
- **Small Text:** Calibri Regular, 9pt minimum (footnotes, references)
- **Line Spacing:** 1.2 for body text, 1.1 for headings
- **Character Spacing:** Standard (0) for body text
- **Paragraph Spacing:** 6pt after paragraphs, 12pt after sections
