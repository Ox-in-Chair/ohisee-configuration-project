## ğŸ¯ PURPOSE & SCOPE

### **Refresher Training Definition**
Refresher training verifies that personnel maintain competency in previously learned skills and knowledge. Unlike initial training, refresher modules:

- **Focus on competency verification** rather than initial learning
- **Emphasize practical demonstration** over theoretical knowledge
- **Address habit degradation** and procedure updates
- **Provide condensed, targeted content** (30-90 minutes vs 90-180 minutes)
- **Include performance-based assessment** with clear pass/fail criteria

### **Target Audience** 
All factory personnel **EXCLUDING senior management modules**:
- Factory workers (65 staff)
- Team leaders (8 staff)
- Packers (12 staff)
- Operators (15 staff)
- Setter operators (6 staff)
- Maintenance technicians (4 staff)
- Production managers (3 staff)

**Total:** 113 personnel across 17 refresher modules
